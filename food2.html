WHAT CAN I DO
It's important to keep a
vigilant eye and critical
mind when considering
food choices
EAT LOCAL
Eating local is an easy
way to reduce your footprint!
However, eating locally
doesn't come with without
some caveats.
EAT VEGGIES
One of the easiest and most
impactful ways of reducing
your carbon footprint can be
accomplished by replacing
and reducing meat consumption
in your diet. Eating veggies
will not only benefit your
carbon footprint, but your
health as well!
EAT SEASONAL
In addition to with eating
locally, eat seasonally as well.
If something is off season,
but grown locally in a
greenhouse, it's carbon
footprint is much larger
than usual.
SUMMARY
Food miles contribute to a larger concept of carbon footprint; food miles are a factor, but it is important to consider other aspects of food production. Production emissions greatly outweigh transportation emissions, so it is important to understand how food is produced, as well as where it is coming from.
